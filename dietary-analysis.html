Dietary Habits and Mental Health Analysis

Analysis of the relationship between dietary habits, mental health, sleep patterns, and academic performance among Indian university students.

Key Findings

  • Strong correlation between diet quality and mental health outcomes
  • Students with healthy diets show significantly lower depression rates
  • Combined effect of good diet and adequate sleep provides best outcomes
  • High-performing students with poor diets show increased depression risk
