Mental Health Among Indian Students

A Statistical Analysis of Factors Affecting Student Well-being in Indian Higher Education

Understanding the Indian Educational Context

India's higher education system is one of the largest in the world, with over 1,000 universities and 42,000 colleges serving more than 37 million students. This massive scale brings unique challenges:

  • Competitive Pressure: With limited seats in top institutions, students face intense academic pressure
  • Financial Challenges: Despite relatively lower tuition compared globally, many students struggle with educational costs
  • Cultural Expectations: High family and societal expectations add to academic stress
  • Work-Study Balance: Many students balance part-time work with studies to support their education
